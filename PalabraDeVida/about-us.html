Features

Sixteen skins

The theme comes with 16 preset colour shemes to choose from - Or maybe you wish to create your own?

360+ icons

A gorgeous collection of retina-ready icons for sharp display on mobile devices with five different sizes.

Social media

Built-in social media tools allowing your customers to engage with your products and share them with ease.

Typography

We have packed La Boutique with a wealth of typography elements - including block quotes.

Support

A dedicated team provides timely and effective support whenever you need a hand getting things right - just shout!

Fully responsive

Mobile first! La Boutique is designed for an optimum user experience on mobile devices leading to higher conversions.

Product reviews

Built-in Facebook comments for your customers to tell the world why they love your products!
